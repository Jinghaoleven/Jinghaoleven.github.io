About

I am currently a Master student in the Department of Automation, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), advised by Prof. Feng Zhao at BIVLab. I obtained my Bachelor degree in the School of Electronic Engineering, XiDian University (XDU) in 2021. My research interests include computer vision and machine learning, especially low-level vision tasks.
