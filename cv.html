About

I am currently a Ph.D. student at the Department of Automation, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), advised by Prof. Feng Zhao at BIVLab. I obtained my Bachelor degree in the School of Electronic Engineering, XiDian University (XDU) in 2021. My current research include reasoning in large language models and Reinforcement Learning, and I previously worked in computer vision, especially generative models and low-level vision tasks.
