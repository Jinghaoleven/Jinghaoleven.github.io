About

I am currently a Ph.D. student at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) and Shanghai Innovation Institute (SII), advised by Prof. Feng Zhao and co-advised by Jiaqi Wang. My current research interests include LLM post-training and reinforcement learning. I am also a research intern in the TGT Program at JD Explore Academy, where I work on JoyAI-VL foundation models.

Previously, I worked on computer vision, particularly generative models and low-level vision. I received my bachelor's degree from the School of Electronic Engineering at Xidian University in 2021, advised by Prof. Zhekun Zheng.
